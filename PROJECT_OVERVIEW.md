# Maskesotta: Project Overview

## üåü The Vision

**Maskesotta** is more than just a web application‚Äîit's a bridge between compassion and need. At its heart, this project embodies the spirit of community care by connecting generous donors with Minnesota residents who need Baklava masks for protection.

The name itself is a playful blend of "Mask" and "Minnesota" (often affectionately called "Minne-sota"), reflecting the project's local focus and its mission to protect Minnesotans.

## üéØ The Problem We're Solving

In times when protective equipment becomes essential, not everyone has equal access. Maskesotta addresses this disparity by creating a simple, dignified way for Minnesota residents to receive free Baklava masks while giving donors an easy, transparent way to help their neighbors.

### The Human Element

This isn't about charity in the traditional sense‚Äîit's about **community solidarity**. The platform removes the stigma often associated with asking for help by:

- **Maintaining dignity**: Recipients simply register; there's no means testing or invasive questions
- **Creating connection**: Donors know their contribution goes directly to a real person in their state
- **Building trust**: The Minnesota zip code verification ensures the system serves its intended community

## ü§ù How It Works

### For Recipients (Minnesota Residents)
1. **Simple Registration**: Fill out a straightforward form with name, address, and email
2. **Verification**: The system validates Minnesota zip codes to ensure fairness
3. **Wait for Matching**: Once a donor sponsors a mask, it ships directly to the recipient
4. **Receive Protection**: A free Baklava mask arrives at their door

### For Donors (Anyone, Anywhere)
1. **Choose to Help**: Visit the platform and decide to make a difference
2. **Donate $25**: A single contribution covers one mask plus shipping
3. **Direct Impact**: The mask goes straight to a verified Minnesota resident
4. **Feel Good**: Know you've helped protect someone in need

## üí° The Philosophy

### Transparency First
Every aspect of Maskesotta is designed to be transparent:
- Clear pricing ($25 per mask, including shipping)
- Straightforward process (no hidden fees or complications)
- Honest communication (recipients know they're on a waiting list)

### Technology as an Enabler
The tech stack isn't chosen for flashiness‚Äîit's chosen for reliability and simplicity:
- **Next.js**: Fast, modern, and maintainable
- **Supabase**: Secure database without complexity
- **Shopify**: Trusted payment processing (planned)

The goal is to get out of the way and let the human connection happen.

### Community-Centric Design
The dual-panel interface isn't just a design choice‚Äîit's a statement. By placing recipients and donors side-by-side on the homepage, the platform visually reinforces that both groups are equally important parts of the same community.
